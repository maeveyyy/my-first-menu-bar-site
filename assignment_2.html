Tipperary

I am from a small town in Tipperary called Cashel and this it the Rock of Cashel a well known historical site.
It is especially nice when the sun is shining, the grass looks greener, the sky looks bluer, the air feels fresher and the sun is warm on your skin. When I was younger my parents used to bring me here when the weather was nice, we used get ice cream from the shop just at the foot of the rock and go for a walk around.

Malaysia

My dad is from Malaysia and this photo is from a beach about an hour drive from his home place.
When my dad was in his later years of secondary school he often went to this beach at the weekend with his friends. They would bring tents and camp at the beach for few nights and have a mini barbeque to cook their food.
During the summer I went to Malsysia with my family to visit our relatives and we got to swim in the warm sea and watch many beautiful sunsets at the beach.
